About Crafted Gamz

Crafted Gamz is a web platform providing access to games, applications, AI services, virtual machines, and more - all directly through your browser.

This platform is also meant for internet freedom. With the on-going, rising censorship in this world all the way from GoGuardian or a school chromebook to Europes' Digital Services Act, this site will try and help you evade that.
